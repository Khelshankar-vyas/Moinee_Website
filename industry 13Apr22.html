Industry Alignment Program

About Project

Skill development is a powerful tool to empower the people and improves their social acceptance. It is complemented by a good economic growth and enhances employability. India is at the verge of being a developed country with a need for a larger number of skilled and knowledgeable people. Industry Alignment program is a ground-breaking step in this direction. It is designed and developed to facilitate I.T.I./Polytechnics/B.Tech/MCA/BCA/MBA /BBA/Graduates/Dropouts students to improve their employability skills and make them industry ready. Under this program, different short term courses/ workshops/ seminars are conducted.
