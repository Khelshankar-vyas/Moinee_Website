Rewards and Recognition

CSR Implementation Agency of the Year 2020-21

Moinee Foundation won the CSR Implementing Agency of the Year 2020-21 Award at the 7th CSR Impact Awards, hosted by Csrbox and Dalmia Bharat Foundation.

Outstanding Performance In Transforming Mass Education Trophy-2021

Moinee Foundation was conferred the "Outstanding Performance In Transforming Mass Education Trophy-2021" in Best Employer Award Competition by 'The Employers Association of Rajasthan'. The award was presented by honorable Loksabha Speaker Mr. Om Birla and Rajasthan Legislative Assembly Speaker Mr. CP Joshi.

eNGO award (South Asia 2019-20)

Moinee Foundation got eNGO award (South Asia ,2019-20) in Education & Learning Category for two flagship initiatives- Project Utkarsh & Community eLibrary.

State CSR Excellence Award - 2019

Winner of State CSR Excellence Award - 2019 for Education category. Moinee Foundation is recognized by Govt. of Rajasthan for its education-related initiatives including Project Utkarsh and eGyankendra, which pan-India serving learners in local languages.

Manthan Award

Moinee Foundation got Manthan Award (South West India, 2014) under the category eNGO (Jury's Special Mention) for the work it did in 12 rural and tribal schools as part of Project Utkarsh pilot in Udaipur District of Rajasthan.

eGyanKendra (Jhalawar – Sumel) included in the book titled ‘New Beginning’ was released by Prime Minister Narendra Modi on the 11 Civil Services Day in New Delhi in 2018. This initiative from Rajasthan has found place among top 62 innovations by different districts in the country.

Project Utkarsh was also included in the book 'New Pathway' published by Department of Administrative Reforms and Public Grievances and released by Prime Minister Narendra Modi. Of the around 900 applications received from different corners of the nation, only 20 have found place in the book.

A letter of appreciation from Shri Ashutosh AT Pednekar, Collector and District Magistrate, Udaipur on successful completion of Project Utkarsh pilot in 12 rural and tribal govt. sec. schools.
